Projects

  • Regular Path Queries Based on Exemplars.

    • User provides a source-destination pair, all paths between which characterise the paths of interest.
    • Biermann-Feldman algorithm is used to infer the regex.
    • Using scale-free and homophily properties of graphs, combined with iterative sampling, we are able to produce destination nodes with greater than 90% accuracy and within half the time compared to baseline approaches.

  • Traffic Density Calculator

    • OpenCV Library was used to make a traffic density calculator from a video feed of a major traffic junction in Delhi. Dynamic and Static Density were calculated, and a variety of optimisation techniques like frame skipping, resolution lowering, were carried out.
